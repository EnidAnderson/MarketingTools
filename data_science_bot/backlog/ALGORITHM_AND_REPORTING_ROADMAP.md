# Algorithm And Reporting Roadmap

## Near-Term (1-2 Weeks)

1. Revenue truth table with duplicate-adjusted and ledger-reconciled totals.
2. Device funnel with uncertainty intervals and practical-significance markers.
3. Retention cohort curves and repeat-purchase survival view.

## Mid-Term (3-6 Weeks)

1. Campaign lift framework with quasi-experimental baselines.
2. Bayesian segment stabilizer for low-volume channels.
3. Forecasting module with confidence calibration tracking.

## Longer-Term (6-12 Weeks)

1. Multi-touch attribution sensitivity engine.
2. Decision simulator with uncertainty-aware budget allocation scenarios.
3. Unified scientific report packet for executive and technical audiences.

## Hard Gating Conditions

- No algorithm promotion without calibration and backtest evidence.
- No executive chart launch without data lineage and uncertainty context.
- No causal recommendation without explicit assumptions and sensitivity checks.
