# Algorithm And Reporting Roadmap

## Near-Term (1-2 Weeks)

1. `DS-TKT-0001` Traffic taxonomy and measurement audit.
2. `DS-TKT-0003` Device and landing-page funnel survival with uncertainty intervals and practical-significance markers.
3. `DS-TKT-0006` Retention cohort curves and repeat-purchase survival view.
4. `DS-TKT-0007` Power gating and minimum detectable effect rules for decision cards.

## Mid-Term (3-6 Weeks)

1. `DS-TKT-0008` Causal opportunity queue and campaign lift framework with quasi-experimental baselines.
2. `DS-TKT-0005` Session quality scoring plus Bayesian stabilizer for low-volume segments.
3. `DS-TKT-0009` Forecasting and anomaly triage with confidence calibration tracking.

## Longer-Term (6-12 Weeks)

1. Attribution sensitivity engine with explicit uncertainty and topology caveats.
2. Decision simulator with uncertainty-aware budget allocation scenarios.
3. `DS-TKT-0010` Unified scientific evidence packet for executive and technical audiences.

## Hard Gating Conditions

- No algorithm promotion without calibration and backtest evidence.
- No executive chart launch without data lineage and uncertainty context.
- No causal recommendation without explicit assumptions and sensitivity checks.
