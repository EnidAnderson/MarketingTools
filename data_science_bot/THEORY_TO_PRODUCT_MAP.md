# Theory To Product Map

This map ties advanced statistical topics to practical analytics features for Nature's Diet.

## Causal Inference

- Product feature: campaign policy lift estimator.
- Method anchor: target-trial framing, propensity weighting, sensitivity to unmeasured confounding.
- Output requirement: effect estimate, interval, assumption diagnostics.

## Hierarchical/Bayesian Modeling

- Product feature: sparse-segment KPI stabilization for device/campaign slices.
- Method anchor: partial pooling and posterior intervals.
- Output requirement: shrunk estimates, uncertainty width, low-volume warnings.

## Survival Analysis

- Product feature: retention hazard dashboard from first session to repeat purchase.
- Method anchor: hazard/survival curves with cohort stratification.
- Output requirement: time-to-event profiles and confidence bands.

## Forecasting And Time-Series

- Product feature: daily revenue forecast with pacing alerts.
- Method anchor: ETS/ARIMA/dynamic regression with backtesting.
- Output requirement: forecast intervals, calibration error, drift markers.

## Experimentation Science

- Product feature: experiment readiness and post-test decision card.
- Method anchor: power analysis, MDE, guardrails, sequential discipline.
- Output requirement: powered/not-powered state and decision recommendation.

## Missing Data And Measurement Error

- Product feature: data quality scorecard and sensitivity panel.
- Method anchor: missingness diagnostics, imputation sensitivity, bias bounds.
- Output requirement: confidence downgrades when missingness bias risk is high.
