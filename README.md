This workspace is designed to help us collaborate on creating exciting and effective marketing campaigns for Nature's Diet Pet. Think of me as your AI-powered "Marketing Architect." I'm here to help you turn your ideas into high-quality marketing materials.

Below is a directory of the files in this workspace, which will help you navigate our projects and understand our workflow.
