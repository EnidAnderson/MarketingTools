# INV-GLOBAL-OPS-002

## Invariant Statement
Critical incidents must execute containment actions within 60 minutes.

## Rationale
Limits blast radius and recovery cost.

## Scope
Security, claim, spend, and authority incidents.

## Enforcement Points
`planning/INCIDENT_RESPONSE_PLAYBOOK.md`, incident logs.

## Evidence of Compliance
Timeline records show containment actions completed within SLA.

## Failure State
Containment SLA missed for critical incident.

## Owner Role
Security Steward.

## Test Requirements
Incident-drill timeline tests validate SLA handling.
