# INV-GLOBAL-EVD-001

## Invariant Statement
Externally-facing claims must map to evidence or explicit caveat.

## Rationale
Maintains trust and auditability of claims.

## Scope
Marketing copy, visuals, demos, and reports.

## Enforcement Points
`planning/RAPID_REVIEW_CELL/logs/*`, `planning/RELEASE_GATES_POLICY.md`.

## Evidence of Compliance
Claim register entries map to support entries or caveat text.

## Failure State
Claim reaches publish path with no evidence/caveat mapping.

## Owner Role
Product Steward.

## Test Requirements
Claim-to-evidence linkage tests and caveat path tests.
