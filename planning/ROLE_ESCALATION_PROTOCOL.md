# Role Escalation Protocol

Effective date: 2026-02-10

## Trigger conditions

1. Conflicting vetoes between roles.
2. Ambiguous authority for safety-critical decision.
3. SLA breach on unresolved role conflict.

## Time-bound path

1. T+0 min: record conflict with involved roles and decision scope.
2. T+30 min: Team Lead convenes Security Steward + Product Steward for safety assessment.
3. T+2 hr: if unresolved, escalate to Platform Architect for technical tie-break input.
4. T+24 hr: unresolved safety-critical conflict triggers mandatory block state until resolved.

## Resolution outputs

1. Assigned accountable role.
2. Decision statement.
3. Evidence and control references.
4. Follow-up remediation task (if applicable).
