## Indicator thresholds

1. Security posture:
- green: no unresolved critical findings; all required scans passing.
- yellow: medium findings or incomplete weekly threat/control review.
- red: critical unresolved finding or failed mandatory gate.
2. Budget discipline:
- green: all runs have envelopes; no unapproved overruns.
- yellow: exceptions active but approved with expiry.
- red: unapproved cap exceedance.
3. Role clarity:
- green: no ambiguous safety-critical decisions.
- yellow: temporary overlap resolved within SLA.
- red: unresolved safety-critical conflict > 24 hours.
4. Evidence quality:
- green: externally-facing claims all supported/caveated.
- yellow: caveat debt queued with owner.
- red: unsupported claim in publish path.
5. Incident readiness:
- green: playbook current and recent drill pass.
- yellow: stale drill but coverage complete.
- red: missing incident class response path.
