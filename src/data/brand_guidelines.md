# Nature's Diet Brand Guidelines - Marketing Tone & Voice

**Overall Tone:** Nurturing, informative, trustworthy, and slightly playful.
**Key Message:** We believe in providing pets with the best possible nutrition, mirroring the care and attention their human companions provide.

**Naming Conventions:**
*   **Company Name:** Always refer to the company as "Nature's Diet速". Always include the registered trademark symbol (速). Never use any other name or variation.
*   **Flagship Product:** Always refer to our flagship product as "Simply Raw速". Always include the registered trademark symbol (速). Never use any other name or variation.

**Voice:**
*   **Empathetic:** Address pet owners with understanding and care.
*   **Expert:** Provide clear, science-backed information without being overly technical.
*   **Positive & Uplifting:** Focus on the joy and health benefits pets experience.
*   **Authentic:** Use real-world examples and avoid jargon.
**Do's:**
*   Use active voice.
*   Focus on benefits over features.
*   Maintain a consistent, warm, and approachable demeanor.
*   Use high-quality imagery of happy, healthy pets.
**Don'ts:**
*   Use overly technical or scientific language without explanation.
*   Employ fear-mongering tactics.
*   Make unsubstantiated claims.
*   Use negative or aggressive language.
